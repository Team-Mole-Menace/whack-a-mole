WHACK-A-MOLE!

Game Rules

Your goal is to whack as many moles in 30 seconds.
Keep in mind there are 2 kinds of mole.

A blue friendly mole will give you a point.
A red deadly mole will take out a point.
You can select the number of moles (from 3 to 8) and your level of difficulty (easy, medium and hard).

Take a deep breath and play your best!
